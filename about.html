Our Story

A legacy of hospitality on the Somali Coast.

Welcome to mareero hotel boosaso

Nestled along the pristine shores of bosaso , our resort was founded with a single vision: to showcase the untold beauty and hospitality of Somalia to the world.


We believe in "Is-fahan" (Understanding) and "Martisoor" (Hospitality). Whether you are visiting for business in the growing port city or seeking a peaceful retreat, Mareero Hotel Bosaso offers a sanctuary of luxury and calm.


Our Mission

To provide world-class unparalleled comfort.

To ensure the safety and privacy of every guest.

To celebrate delicious local and international cuisine.
